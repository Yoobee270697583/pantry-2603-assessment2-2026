### "Planned Meals" - Loose ordered list (not calendar-locked) of meals the user intends to cook soon - Add recipes from library, suggestions, or custom recipes - Drag-to-reorder for shifting priorities (e.g. expiring ingredients) - Feeds directly into the dynamic shopping list - **Per-meal card** — tapping/clicking the meal card always opens the full recipe spec - **Two primary buttons per meal:** - **Mark as cooked** — allocates the meal as cooked; dynamically updates Pantry inventory by removing the ingredients used, and moves the meal into Cooked Meals - **Delete** — removes the meal from the planned-to-cook queue (no pantry effect) {% endblock %}
